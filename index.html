Fadel Kassab

Who's Fadel?

Fadel is a multi-talented and creative individual with a deep passion for Computer Science, particularly in Theoretical Computer Science, Bioinformatics, and Artificial Intelligence. Currently, he is pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the American University of Beirut (AUB). His interests extend to Mathematics, Logic, and Philosophy, reflecting his love for intellectual exploration. Beyond his technical pursuits, Fadel is an accomplished Arabic poet who has been sharing his work at events in Lebanon since a young age. At his core, he is an enthusiastic and lifelong learner, driven by an insatiable curiosity.

He's someone who cherishes the simple joys of life—sipping coffee while listening to the melodies of birds as the sun rises over the horizon. A true nature enthusiast, he finds peace in its beauty. In his free time, he enjoys solving Rubik’s cubes and all kinds of puzzles, diving into new topics, or creating something unique. Open-minded and thoughtful, he’s the person you turn to for deep, meaningful conversations, always striving to stay as close to the truth as possible in everything he does.
